Authorization (Menu Permissions)

To perform certain alarm-related functions, such as viewing, acknowledging, and responding to alarms, or changing the color scheme and audio tone of the alarm priority, your P2000 operator account must be configured to allow it. For example, one operator may have alarm visibility while an operator with a different set of menu permissions may not be allowed to view alarms.

For more information on configuring menu permissions, refer to the P2000 Software User Manual (Part No. 24-10685-157).
