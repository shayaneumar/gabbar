Acknowledging Alarms

When you acknowledge an alarm, you are indicating within the P2000 Alarm Manager that you have seen the alarm and will track it until it becomes resolved.

You can acknowledge any alarms with a status of Pending if your P2000 user permissions allow it. See Alarm Life Cycle for more information on acknowledging alarms within an alarm’s life cycle.

You can acknowledge a single alarm or multiple alarms at once.

Once you acknowledge an alarm, the alarm status changes to Acknowledged and the system adds the action to the alarm history. See Alarm Details and History for more information.

To acknowledge a single alarm:

1.     Locate the alarm you wish to acknowledge.

2.     Click the Acknowledge button on the row of the alarm to be acknowledged.

To acknowledge multiple alarms:

1.     Select the check box next to each alarm you wish to acknowledge.

2.     Click the Acknowledge button at the top left of the page.
