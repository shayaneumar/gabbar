Completing Alarms

By completing an alarm, you are confirming that the alarm no longer requires tracking and can be considered closed, although it will still remain visible in the alarm list (see Removing Alarms for information on removing alarms from the list).

You can complete an alarm under various conditions, as described in Alarm Life Cycle.

You can complete a single alarm or multiple alarms at once. Once you complete an alarm, the status changes to Completed.

To complete a single alarm:

1.     Locate the alarm you wish to complete.

2.     Expand the alarm details by performing one of the following actions:

●      Click the twisty arrow to the left of the alarm.

●      Click the Details/Respond link to the right of the alarm.

3.     Click Complete.

The P2000 Alarm Manager records the action in the alarm history.

To complete multiple alarms:

1.     Select the check box next to each alarm you wish to complete.

2.     Click drop-down arrow next to the Acknowledge button at the top left of the page and select Complete.

The P2000 Alarm Manager records the action in the alarm history of each alarm selected.
