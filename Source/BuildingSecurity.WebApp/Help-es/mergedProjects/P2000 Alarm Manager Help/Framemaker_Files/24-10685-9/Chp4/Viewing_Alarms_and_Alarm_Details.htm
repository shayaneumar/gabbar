P2000 Web User Interface Help

Viewing Alarms and Alarm Details

When you log on to the P2000 Alarm Manager, the system displays all currently active alarms that you are allowed to view based on your P2000 permissions, with the most recent alarms listed first.

To view additional alarms not currently visible on the screen:

Use the scroll bar and scroll to the bottom of the page. The P2000 Alarm Manager loads and displays more alarms onto the page as you continue to scroll.

For information on the alarm data displayed on the Alarm Manager page, see Alarm Visibility and Notification.

You can also view alarm details and a history of previous actions for a particular alarm.

To view alarm details and the alarm history:

Expand the alarm details by performing one of the following actions:

●      Click the twisty arrow to the left of the alarm.

●      Click the Details/Respond link to the right of the alarm.

For an explanation of the alarm detail and history information, see Alarm Details and History.

To close the alarm details, click Close or the twisty arrow.
