Viewing Alarms and Alarm History

When viewing the Alarm Manager page, the system displays all currently active alarms that you are allowed to view based on your P2000 permissions, with the most recent alarms listed first.

To view additional alarms not currently visible on the screen:

Use the scroll bar and scroll to the bottom of the page. The P2000 Alarm Manager loads and displays more alarms onto the page as you continue to scroll.

For information on the alarm data displayed on the Alarm Manager page, see Alarm Visibility and Notification.

You can also view a history of previous actions for a particular alarm.

To view the alarm history:

Click the Show History >> link.

For an explanation of the alarm history information, see Alarm History.

Note:   When you expand to show an alarm’s history, the Alarm Manager does not display any new alarms in the list until you hide the alarm history. However, the total alarm count is updated if you have expanded the alarm history.
