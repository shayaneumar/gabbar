Standard Reports

The P2000 ARS comes with a set of standard reports. If necessary, you can configure custom reports and use those as well.

The standard reports are:

●      Alarm Activity: Lists all alarm activities, including acknowledgments, responses, clearing of alarms, and so on.

●      Alarm History: Lists all alarms stored on the P2000 Server.

●      Alarm Summary: Provides a historical summary of alarms based on category, type, description, and so on.

●      Area Transaction History: Lists all badge in and out transactions stored on the P2000 Server for a specified area.

●      Audit Trail: Lists the menu items selected by an operator during a specific time and date. The report is time stamped and monitors day-to-day activity of an operator.

●      Cardholder Transaction: Lists the access/denial transaction of a badge.

●      Guard Tour Transaction: Lists all tour transactions performed by a guard during a tour of the facility.

●      Intrusion Transaction: Lists the recorded historical activity of various intrusion-related events.

●      Muster Analysis: Lists the personnel who were in a muster zone during a drill or an emergency.

●      Transaction: Lists the recorded history of system elements (panels, terminals, inputs, outputs, host, and so on) and the activity of those elements.
